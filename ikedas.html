Ikedas

The Ikedas are the Celestial Royal family, both of Heaven and Hell.

These are the people with titles such as God, The Devil and The Head Grim Reaper. They mostly live on Earth now, acting as humans while also doing their royal duties.

Angels, inhabitants of The Kingdom of Heaven are ruled over by God. A title passed down through Angel royalty. Demons are ruled over by The Monarch of their specific kingdom: Ruby, Diamond and Emerald Quartz, as well as The Emperor of Hell also known as The Devil.
